About Me

Hi! I'm Kieran, a games programmer based in Liverpool. Currently, I'm diving into my second year of Games Programming at SAE Institute Liverpool. Ever since I was young, my heart has beat for horror games, especially those created with Unreal Engine. This passion was sparked by my own cherished memories of playing them. My journey has been all about pushing the boundaries of fear and creativity in the gaming universe. Along the way, I’ve developed a knack for crafting experiences that genuinely thrill and captivate players.
Below, you’ll find a collection of projects I’ve poured my heart into, alongside some fun personal ventures. I take immense pride in learning new skills and embracing challenges head-on. Whether it’s fine-tuning a game feature or diving into new technologies, my goal is always to realise the creative vision to its fullest potential. There’s something irresistible about the technical side of gaming that keeps me endlessly curious and motivated. Occasionally, I dabble in web development, like the creation of this very site. If you have any questions or just want to chat, feel free to reach out!
